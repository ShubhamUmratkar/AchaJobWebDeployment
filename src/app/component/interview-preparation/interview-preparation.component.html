Interview Preparation Roadmap

Phase 1: Understand the Process

1. Interview Types

  • Technical Interviews: Coding/Subject-based.
  • HR Interviews: Behavioral and situational questions.
  • Case Study/Problem-Solving Rounds: Consulting roles.

2. Research the Company

  • Read about the company’s culture, products, and recent achievements.
  • Review interview experiences on platforms like Glassdoor.

Phase 2: Preparing for Technical Interviews

1. Core Knowledge

  • Review fundamentals of your domain and frequently asked questions.

2. Coding Practice (for tech roles)

  • Platforms: LeetCode, HackerRank, Codeforces, GeeksforGeeks.
  • Focus on problem-solving, debugging, and optimized code.

3. Behavioral Questions

  • Use the STAR format to answer questions like:
  • Tell me about yourself, Why should we hire you, What’s your biggest achievement?

Phase 3: Mock Interviews and Feedback

1. Mock Interviews

  • Participate in mock technical and HR interviews.
  • Use platforms like InterviewBit, Pramp, or college resources.

2. Real-Time Problem Solving

  • Practice whiteboard coding or pair programming.

3. Feedback and Improvement

  • Record mock sessions and review areas of improvement.

Phase 4: Final Touch

1. Interview Etiquette

  • Dress formally, maintain professionalism, and communicate clearly.

2. Confidence Building

  • Visualize success, stay positive, and manage stress with mindfulness techniques.

3. Preparation Kit

  • Carry extra copies of your resume, a pen, and a notebook.
  • Review key concepts and notes a day before the interview.
